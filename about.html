Our Origins

Fading Minds began in 2019 with five people passionate about design, development, film, storytelling and technology. They shared the belief that creativity was the most powerful tool a business could use. At first, they focused on two things – filmmaking to satisfy their creativity and the design and build of a broadcast quality live-streaming system to satisfy their urge to innovate.

Web design and experience design came next, leading Fading Minds to pioneer our multi-sensorial brand identity system that defines graphics, sound, motion, texture and emotion. We call this the Brand Being ™. It’s this DNA that has shaped who we are today.

We work with people we believe in

We know there is no such thing as a perfect company, but we also know that organizations with shared aims can create immense positive change. We are actively seeking founders, leaders and organizations who have similar ambitions to Fading Minds. And once we find each other, we commit 100%.

One studio, connected

We believe that collaboration is the lifeblood of creativity. It’s not easy – but we do it every day. Anyone who works in a global organisation will know it’s a struggle that has to be won.We build teams across our four studios that bring together the right experience and expertise to solve the challenge in hand. We stay connected through screens, we move between studios and work in our clients’ locations – whatever the project demands.
